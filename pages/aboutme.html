About

Fresh from completing an intensive software engineering graduate program at Mable Technologies, Michelle emerges as a versatile visionary, equipped with the latest tools in Angular, Ruby on Rails and AWS Infrastructure.

Now driven by a thirst for innovation, Michelle is ready to pivot towards UX/UI design, eager to reimagine how aesthetics and functionality intersect. Her blend of software engineering prowess and business acumen positions her uniquely to create compelling and user-friendly experiences.

"I have a passion for problem solving and am particularly interested in customer experience and product design. For me, empathy is key to being an awesome designer and developer, understanding exactly what the user is experiencing when they use your product is of the utmost importance and coming up with innovative ideas for improving it for them inspires me.

User testing and research fascinate me. It's about developing a product that stands out from your competitors and exceeds user expectations."
