About Me

I'm a former fashion designer, product developer and business owner turned junior software developer. After working for over 20 years in the fashion industry including owning my own fashion label and boutique, I was ready for a new challenge...

Today I work for Mable as a Graduate Software Engineer. Being a part of an organisation that strives to enhance the lives of elderly or disabled individuals by empowering them to make decisions about their own support is motivating to me.

I have a passion for problem solving and am particularly interested in the customer experience and product design.
In my spare time I enjoy spending time with my family and friends, being out in nature, staying up-to-date with the latest catwalk fashion shows and sketching.
